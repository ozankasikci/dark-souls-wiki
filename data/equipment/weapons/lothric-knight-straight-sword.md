# Lothric Knight Straight Sword

The Lothric Knight Straight Sword (LKSS) is widely considered one of the best straight swords in Dark Souls III. With superior reach, excellent scaling, and 110 critical modifier, it outclasses most weapons in its category.

## Why It's Meta

- **Longest** straight sword reach
- **110** critical modifier (rare for straight swords)
- S scaling in DEX when Sharp infused
- Low stamina consumption
- Versatile moveset

## Build Optimization

### Sharp DEX Build (SL 120)
- **VIG**: 40
- **END**: 40
- **DEX**: 70-80
- **STR**: 11 (minimum)
- Sharp +10: 428 AR

### Quality Build (SL 120)
- **VIG**: 35
- **END**: 35
- **STR**: 40
- **DEX**: 40
- Refined +10: 415 AR

### Dark Build (SL 120)
- **VIG**: 35
- **END**: 35
- **INT**: 30
- **FTH**: 30
- Dark +10: 441 AR (split damage)

## Critical Damage

### Backstab/Riposte Modifier
- LKSS: **110**
- Other straight swords: **100**
- 10% more critical damage

### Hornet Ring Synergy
- Base critical: 110
- With Hornet Ring: 143
- Devastating ripostes
- One-shot potential

## PvP Applications

### Tournament Staple
- Consistent 2-hit combos
- Excellent running R1
- Shield chip damage
- Pairs with any offhand

### Advanced Techniques
1. **R1 → R1 → R2**: Mix-up combo
2. **Running R1 → R1**: Roll catch
3. **Backstep → R1**: Spacing tool
4. **R2 → R1**: Delayed combo

## Farming Guide

### Best Locations
1. **High Wall of Lothric**
   - Tower bonfire
   - 3 knights nearby
   - ~2% drop rate

2. **Lothric Castle**
   - Dragon Barracks bonfire
   - Multiple knights
   - Higher drop rate

### Farming Setup
- **Symbol of Avarice**: +100% item discovery
- **Crystal Sage's Rapier**: +50% (offhand)
- **Gold Serpent Ring +3**: +115%
- **Rusted Coins**: +50% (temporary)
