# Black Knight Sword

The Black Knight Sword is a powerful greatsword wielded by the Black Knights who followed Lord Gwyn into battle against the demons. These weapons were charred black in the Kiln of the First Flame and retain special effectiveness against demon-type enemies.

## Acquisition

Dropped by Black Knights that wield this weapon (approximately 20% drop rate):
- **Undead Burg**: One Black Knight near the tower before Taurus Demon
- **Undead Parish**: One Black Knight in the tower
- **Darkroot Basin**: One Black Knight patrolling near the Grass Crest Shield
- **Kiln of the First Flame**: Multiple Black Knights (respawn, endgame)

Increase drop rate with:
- Covetous Gold Serpent Ring
- Soft Humanity (up to 10)
- Symbol of Avarice

## Special Properties

- **20% bonus damage** against all demon-type enemies
- Cannot be buffed with resins or magic
- Cannot be infused
- High base damage makes it effective even without scaling investment
- Unique moveset with wide swings

## Moveset

- **One-handed R1**: Wide horizontal sweeps
- **One-handed R2**: Overhead slam
- **Two-handed R1**: Vertical slashes with increased range
- **Two-handed R2**: Powerful thrust with knockback

## Combat Effectiveness

### Against Demons (20% bonus)
Particularly effective against:
- Taurus Demon
- Capra Demon
- Demon Firesage
- Centipede Demon
- Bed of Chaos
- All demon-type enemies in Lost Izalith

### General Use
- High base damage rewards strength investment
- Good stagger potential on most enemies
- Wide swings useful for crowd control

## Upgrades

Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite:
- Base → +1 (1 Twinkling Titanite)
- +1 → +2 (1 Twinkling Titanite)
- +2 → +3 (2 Twinkling Titanite)
- +3 → +4 (2 Twinkling Titanite)
- +4 → +5 (4 Twinkling Titanite)

At +5: 330 base damage

## Notes

- Very popular for early-game farming runs
- Getting it from the Undead Burg Black Knight can trivialize early content
- Popular for speedruns when obtained early
- One of the best greatswords in the game
- Pairs well with high-strength builds
