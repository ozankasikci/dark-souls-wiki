# Havel's Ring

## Description
The ring of Havel the Rock, one of Gwyn's trusted knights. Havel despised magic, and his ring greatly increases equipment load.

## Effects
- **Equipment Load**: +50% increase to maximum equip load
- Allows wearing heavier armor while maintaining mobility
- Essential for heavy armor builds

## Stats
- **Weight**: 1.5
- **Durability**: -
- **Equip Load Boost**: +50%

## Location
- **Undead Burg Tower**: Dropped by Havel the Rock
- Access the tower between Undead Burg and Darkroot Basin
- Requires **Master Key** (starting gift) or **Watchtower Basement Key**
- The tower is at the bottom of the stairs before the Taurus Demon boss
- Alternative access: From Darkroot Basin near the Hydra

## Obtaining
1. Unlock the tower door with Master Key or Watchtower Basement Key
2. Descend the spiral staircase
3. Defeat Havel the Rock (very difficult early game)
4. **Warning**: Don't let Havel fall off the ledge or you lose the ring!

## Combat Tips for Havel
- Circle around and backstab his slow attacks
- Use poison (Dung Pies, Poison Mist)
- Lure him out of the tower for more room
- Very high damage - one-shot for most low-level characters

## Notes
- Essential ring for strength builds using heavy weapons and armor
- Allows fat-rolling builds to achieve medium or fast rolls
- Very popular ring choice across all build types
- Can be obtained very early with Master Key starting gift
- Havel also drops his Dragon Tooth and Havel's Greatshield in Anor Londo (separate location)
