# Hornet Ring

## Description
One of the special rings granted to the four knights of Gwyn. The Hornet Ring belonged to Lord's Blade Ciaran, the assassin who loved Artorias.

## Effects
- Increases critical attack damage by 30%
- Works with backstabs, ripostes, and plunging attacks
- Stacks with other damage multipliers

## Stats
- **Weight**: 0.3
- **Durability**: -
- **Critical Boost**: +30%

## Location
- **Darkroot Garden**: Behind the gravestone of Knight Artorias
- Located in the center of Great Grey Wolf Sif's boss arena
- On a corpse near Artorias's grave marker
- Must enter Sif's arena to retrieve (does not require defeating Sif)

## How to Obtain
1. Access Darkroot Garden (from Undead Parish via Andre's area)
2. Open the Crest of Artorias door (20,000 souls from Andre) OR
3. Circle around through Darkroot Basin
4. Enter Sif's boss arena
5. Grab the ring from behind the gravestone
6. Can leave arena if you haven't triggered Sif yet

## Notes
- Essential ring for critical-focused builds
- Particularly effective with weapons that have high critical multipliers (daggers, rapiers)
- Popular choice for PvP builds focused on backstabs and ripostes
- One of four special rings of Gwyn's knights (alongside Leo, Hawk, and Wolf Rings)
- The grave belongs to Artorias, Ciaran's beloved knight
- Ciaran left her ring at his grave as a memorial
