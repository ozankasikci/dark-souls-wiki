# Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring

## Description
A special ring granted to only the most accomplished sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School. The ring is engraved with a bellowing dragon, and increases the damage of sorceries.

## Effects
- Increases sorcery damage by 20%
- Does not affect pyromancy or miracles
- Essential for sorcery builds

## Stats
- **Weight**: 0.1
- **Durability**: -
- **Sorcery Boost**: +20%

## Location
- **Purchased from Griggs of Vinheim** for 20,000 souls
- Griggs is found in the Lower Undead Burg (rescue him by buying the Residence Key)
- After rescue, he moves to Firelink Shrine where he sells sorceries and this ring

## Notes
- Essential ring for any sorcery-focused build
- Pairs well with the Crown of Dusk for maximum sorcery damage
- One of the most important rings for Intelligence builds
