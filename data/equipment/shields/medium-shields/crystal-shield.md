# Crystal Shield

## Description

A straight shield entirely crystallized, with its creation shrouded in mystery. The crystallization enhances its defensive capabilities but also makes the shield fragile and irreparable.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Physical Defense | 100% |
| Magic Defense | 80% |
| Fire Defense | 80% |
| Lightning Defense | 80% |
| Stability | 60 |
| Durability | 250 |
| Weight | 4.0 |
| Strength Requirement | 12 |

## Special Properties

- Cannot be repaired by any means
- Cannot parry (shield bash instead)
- Blocks 100% poison, toxin and bleeding (does not work in PvP)

## Location

- **Purchased from Domhnall of Zena** for 4,000 souls
- Domhnall moves to Firelink Shrine after ringing both bells

## Notes

- Excellent all-around defense, especially against magic
- Cannot be repaired, making it a temporary shield
- Best used for specific encounters rather than general exploration
- Its crystal nature reflects its connection to Seath's experiments
