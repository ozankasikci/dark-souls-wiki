# Cleansing Greatshield

## Description
The steel greatshield used by Knight Artorias, who succumbed to the Abyss. Artorias, deeply scarred by the Abyss, used this to form a barrier to protect his compatriot Sif. Although this drained the shield, its magic defense remains high.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Physical Defense | 80.0 |
| Magic Defense | 80.0 |
| Fire Defense | 80.0 |
| Lightning Defense | 70.0 |
| Stability | 77 |
| Durability | 300 |
| Weight | 14.5 |
| Strength Requirement | 31 |

## Special Properties
- 100% resistance against status effects from enemy attacks
- Can interact with ghosts (like cursed weapons)
- Lightest greatshield option for blocking dark magic

## Location
- Artorias of the Abyss DLC only
- Chasm of the Abyss: Fall through illusory floor, follow Alvina past Humanity Phantoms to hidden room
- Rescue young Sif from the trap, and he leaves the shield behind when he warps away
- Can be obtained before or after defeating Manus

## Notes
- Weakest physical defense of all greatshields (80%)
- Upgrades with Twinkling Titanite to +5
- Stability does not increase when upgraded
- Inferior to Greatshield of Artorias in most respects, but much lighter
