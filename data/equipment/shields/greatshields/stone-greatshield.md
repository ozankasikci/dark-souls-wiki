# Stone Greatshield

## Description
Mossy round greatshield used by the guardian of the Darkroot Garden, the Stone Knight. The Stone Knight is a creation of ancient magic, and this shield is imbued with the same power, but is also extremely heavy.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Physical Defense | 100.0 |
| Magic Defense | 80.0 |
| Fire Defense | 75.0 |
| Lightning Defense | 65.0 |
| Stability | 80 |
| Durability | 150 |
| Weight | 20.0 |
| Strength Requirement | 38 |

## Special Properties
- 100% physical block
- High magic defense (80% - tied 2nd with Crest Shield and Cleansing Greatshield)
- Third highest stability among greatshields (after Greatshield of Artorias and Havel's)

## Location
- Dropped by Stone Knights in Darkroot Garden
- Rare drop (2% chance)
- Stone Knights respawn, allowing for farming

## Notes
- Excellent for magic-heavy encounters (Moonlight Butterfly)
- One of the heaviest shields available (20.0)
- High strength requirement (38) limits early-game use
