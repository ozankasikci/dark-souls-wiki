# Dragon Build

The **Dragon Build** embraces the Path of the Dragon, utilizing dragon form transformation and dragon-themed weapons for a unique bestial playstyle.

## Overview

This build centers around the Dragon Torso transformation, which provides powerful unarmed attacks and a fire breath ability. Combined with dragon weapons, it offers a completely unique Dark Souls experience.

## Starting Class

**Warrior** for optimal stats:
- Balanced STR/DEX
- Low INT/FAI
- Good starting gear

## Dragon Transformation

### Requirements
1. Join Path of the Dragon covenant
2. Obtain Dragon Head Stone
3. Obtain Dragon Torso Stone
4. Use stones while unequipped

### Dragon Form Benefits
- Base 400 AR unarmed damage
- Fire breath attack
- Roar force effect
- Unique appearance

### Dragon Form Drawbacks
- NO armor allowed
- No defense bonuses
- Vulnerable to all damage
- Cannot wear helmets

## Dragon Weapons

### Primary Options
1. **Dragon King Greataxe** - AOE special
   - 380 base damage
   - 50 STR requirement
   - R2 AOE attack

2. **Dragon Greatsword** - Thrust option
   - 390 base damage
   - Ultra greatsword class
   - From dragon tail

3. **Dragon Bone Fist** - Fist weapon
   - Unique moveset
   - Shoryuken R2
   - A/A scaling

### Alternative Weapons
- **Obsidian Greatsword** - Kalameet tail
- **Priscilla's Dagger** - Bleed option
- **Moonlight Greatsword** - INT option

## Combat Strategy

### Dragon Form Combat
1. Use roar to stagger
2. Follow with fire breath
3. Punish with claws
4. Mix in weapon attacks

### Weapon Combat
1. Abuse special attacks
2. Trade with hyper armor
3. Use reach advantage
4. AOE for crowds
