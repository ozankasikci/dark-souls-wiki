Zweihander

Ultra Greatsword

Physical Damage: 130
Critical: 100
Weight: 10.0
Durability: 200
Str Req: 24
Dex Req: 10

Description

The Zweihander is an ultra greatsword, a type of weapon that emphasizes high damage and long reach at the cost of swing speed and high stamina consumption. Despite its German name, it actually more closely resembles a Scottish Claymore.

This weapon has become iconic in the Dark Souls community for its effectiveness and relatively low stat requirements, making it a popular choice for both new and experienced players.

Location

The Zweihander can be found in the Graveyard area near Firelink Shrine. From the bonfire, head towards the cliff edge and look for a path leading down to the graveyard. The weapon is located on a corpse among the tombstones.

Be cautious when retrieving it, as the skeletons in this area can be quite dangerous for low-level characters.

Notes & Tips

  • Has a unique strong attack that knocks down most humanoid enemies
  • Can be buffed with resins and spells
  • Excellent for crowd control due to its wide swings
  • Popular choice for "Giant Dad" builds
  • Can be obtained very early in the game with some careful maneuvering
